About Me

Hello! I’m Luka Metias, a student at UMass Lowell exploring web design and user interfaces. This site is my first GUI homework project. My goals are to practice clean, semantic HTML5, how to style with CSS, and write documentation that clearly explains what I did and why. I’m especially interested in accessibility, typography, and simple layouts that look good on phones and laptops. Outside of class, I enjoy small coding projects, reading about technology trends as well as new cars.

On this page I have some examples of structured content with headings, working links, a styled table in the other tab, an image of a car that’s also a link. I wrote this content to be meaningful rather than a random list of tags.
